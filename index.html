About

My name is Hazel Fathoni Friandra. This is a project I developed for the use of Augmented Reality (AR), which is a technology that overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing our perception of reality. In this project, I have explored the potential of AR to create immersive and interactive experiences. Dive into this project to see how I applied AR technology and skills I developed during my academic journey. Thank you for taking the time to explore this important part of my educational experience.
